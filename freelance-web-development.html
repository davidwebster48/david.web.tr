When you look at my freelance work, please remember that it was done within my clients’ budgets, according to my clients’ requirements, and sometimes fitting in with their existing web sites.

Eternal Ingredients – Cookbooks created for you, by you

I created the part of the website where users can create cookbooks, invite others to contribute to their books, manager their recipe libraries and order copies of the books.

I also did some customisations and configuration to the WordPress marketing side of the website.

meYOU - The Free Online Billboard for University Students and Staff

I worked with graphic designer Robyn Kyberd to launch this site for my client, Ryan. Since then I have done a large number of customisations to this Joomla based site, to add features and adjust how things work. You can read the story behind meYOU, from the site’s owner.

Testize - Cross Browser Testing

I wrote the front-end code for side by side image comparison, with synchronised scrolling and zooming. I also wrote the front-end code for selecting parts of the image and adding notes.

indiemunity - A platform for marketing and discovering great music, equipped with a fully functional music store

I built this site from scratch, to look similar to a previous version. I made use of the jQuery Address plugin to create a one-page app so that the music player would not be interrupted on navigation, and the pages are bookmarkable and the browser’s back and forward buttons work.

The site integrates with PayPal Adaptive Payments so that when a customer makes a purchase the funds go directly to the artist.

FEDANT & GRCCT

These sites allow visitors to search lists of registered providers of health services. The owner of this site was concerned about the lists of providers getting harvested by screen-scrapers. I added a number of anti-screen scraper features, including captcha, displaying sensitive details as images instead of text and rate-limiting.
