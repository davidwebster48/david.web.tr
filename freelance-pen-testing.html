My passion is web application security. I love looking at how websites work, looking for weaknesses and fixing them.

During my ten years of commercial IT experience, I have developed a deep understanding of how the web works. I have worked on websites for small start-ups to multi-billion dollar investment companies and had the opportunity to learn from some of Australia's leading IT security experts.

I will look for the weaknesses on your site that a hacker could use to deface your site, steal your customer data or send spam from your mail server.

I will use a combination of automated tools and manual techniques to find vulnerabilities in your website, and I will take the time to explain them to you in plain English, discuss with you to decide which vulnerabilities should be fixed, and I can fix them.

I have experience with a wide range of technologies and frameworks, including PHP, Spring, JSP, ASP, .NET, Perl, WordPress and others.

I am a regular contributor on security.stackexchange.com.

I am a member of the Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP).

I am a tester on Bugcrowd.

In 2013 I completed the Web App Penetration Testing and Ethical Hacking course delivered by SANS.

Please contact me and we can discuss your needs. I am based in Melbourne's South East suburbs.
